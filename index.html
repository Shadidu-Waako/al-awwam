Zakat

Zakat is more than just charity; it is a divine obligation and a pillar of Islam that purifies wealth and uplifts the less fortunate. Your Zakat contributions help provide food, education, healthcare, and essential support to those in need. Give with sincerity, and together, we can make a difference in the lives of many.
