Our Mission

Al-Awwam Foundation, Change a life

To uplift vulnerable communities in Uganda by providing essential humanitarian aid, promoting education, improving healthcare, and fostering sustainable development through partnership and community-driven initiatives

Our Vision

A self sustaining and empowered community where every individual has access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, fostering a future of dignity and prosperity for all
