Our Gallery

Kurban

Our Kurban program helps distribute meat to those in need during special religious occasions. We share meat from sacrificed animals with families who may not have access to it otherwise. This tradition of giving during religious festivals brings joy and unity to communities, ensuring everyone can partake in the celebration.

Food Pack Distribution

Our Food Packages program gives food to families who need it. We provide essential food items to help them eat well. Giving out food helps families who may not have enough to eat. It's a way to support them and make sure they have what they need to live a better life.

Iftar and Orphans Feeding

Our Feeding Program is dedicated to providing nutritious meals to those in need. Through both short-term interventions and long-term projects, our Charity Foundation aims to alleviate hunger and empower communities. We are driven by our determination, passion, and the guidance of Allah, striving each day to make a positive impact on the lives of others.

More Causes

We provide Zakat to those in need, upholding Islamic values and promoting compassion. Our water well construction projects bring clean drinking water to thirsty communities. By building mosques, we foster spiritual growth, community worship, and unity. Together, these initiatives empower vulnerable populations and strengthen community bonds.
